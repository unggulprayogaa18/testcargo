MISSION PLANNING & COMPLIANCE

RevNAV: THE SORA COMPLIANCE ENGINE

A complete cargo system starts long before takeoff. RevNAV automates regulatory risk by converting complex JARUS SORA requirements into actionable data layers, de-risking your operations before the aircraft even leaves the ground.

AUTOMATED GROUND BUFFERS

Automatically generates optimised safety zones and containment buffers tailored to your specific airframe performance and flight termination reliability.

TACTICAL ASSURANCE VALIDATION

Executes an automated 6-point pre-flight validation covering critical safety factors, including high-resolution 3D terrain collision and C2 link feasibility modeling.

INTELLIGENT POPULATION DATA

Calculates dynamic "No-Fly" zones and Adjacent Area metrics by analyzing real-time max/average population density against desired SAIL requirements.

FLEET & TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT

RevOPS: UNIFIED TACTICAL CONTROL

Scaling a cargo operation requires more than just adding aircraft. RevOPS acts as the central nervous system of your fleet, providing advanced UAS Traffic Management (UTM) and multi-vehicle control to integrate crewed and uncrewed traffic safely at scale.

CENTRALISED ROUTING & SCHEDULING

An industry-leading algorithm that balances demand, capacity, and fairness, allowing operators to prioritize preferences like on-time arrival or minimum distance.

MULTI-VEHICLE FLEET MANAGEMENT

Empowers a single operator to oversee multiple autonomous missions simultaneously via a unified MAVLink connection—a true force-multiplier for your logistics network.

DYNAMIC DECONFLICTION

Seamlessly integrates with our onboard Detect and Avoid (DAA) payload to execute dynamic rerouting and tactical deconfliction in complex, shared airspaces.

ONBOARD SURVEILLANCE

DETECT AND AVOID
(DAA) PAYLOAD

True Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) operations require safe separation. Our in-house miniaturised DAA payload employs real-time computer vision and neural networks to detect, track, and avoid both cooperative and uncooperative aircraft in complex airspaces.
