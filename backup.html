OUR COMMITMENT

MORE THAN JUST CARGO DELIVERY

RevAero’s RACS incorporates our in-house designed and developed Detect and Avoid (DAA) and UAS Traffic Management (UTM) systems, along with our broader fleet of UAS to provide an end-to-end operational middle-mile and last-mile cargo delivery for payloads up to 55 kg, over commercially viable ranges operating for up to 4 hours.

BRIDGING THE INFRASTRUCTURE GAP

Delivering into remote areas, bridging the gap for sites disadvantaged by absent infrastructure. Deployable across the Asia-Pacific as a permanent logistics solution or an urgent response to Humanitarian Aid / Disaster Recovery (HADR) scenarios.

HARSH ENVIRONMENT CAPABLE

Our flagship aircraft is capable of operating in harsh weather conditions, carrying a significant payload with applications in military logistics, humanitarian assistance, and disaster relief scenarios.
